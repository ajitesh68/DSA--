1. Goal
Humein har row mein woh boundary dhundni hai jahan positive numbers khatam hote hain aur negative shuru hote hain.

2. Logic (Divide & Conquer)
Puri row check karne ki jagah hum beech (mid) ka element dekhte hain:

Agar mid negative hai:
Iska matlab iske right side waale saare elements pakka negative hain.
Lekin ho sakta hai iske left mein bhi kuch negative numbers ho.
Toh hum mid ko save kar lete hain aur left side check karte hain (high = mid - 1).

Agar mid positive hai:
Iska matlab yahan tak sab theek hai, koi negative nahi mila.
Negative numbers sirf right side ho sakte hain.
Toh hum right side move karte hain (low = mid + 1).

3. Result
Jab loop khatam hota hai, humein pehla negative index mil chuka hota hai.
Total Negatives = Row ki length - Pehla Negative Index

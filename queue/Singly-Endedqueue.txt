The "Strike Your Head" Intuition (The Merry-Go-Round): Picture a queue as a straight line that you artificially bend into a circle. 
To count around this circle, take a person from the front and immediately push them to the very back. Do this "front-to-back" pass exactly
k−1 times to let those people survive. When the k-th person reaches the front, they are eliminated—just pop() them out and do not put 
them back. You simply keep spinning this loop and throwing away every k-th person until only the final winner remains!
